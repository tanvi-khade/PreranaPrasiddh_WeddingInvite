Prerana & Prasiddh

We met in college and became best friends first, because apparently we like to do things the long way. For three years, we laughed, studied and built a friendship that neither of us realized was quietly turning into something more. Somewhere between endless conversations and movie nights, we recognized our favorite place was simply together.

That friendship became the foundation for the best years of our lives. It carried us through cross-country moves, countless movie nights and our unofficial role as restaurant critics; each day was anything but ordinary. We learned that the joy was never just in what we were doing, but in doing it together. This was the life we wanted to keep choosing.

Now it feels like the story we began years ago is ready to keep unfolding. Rooted in respect, trust and love, it’s a story that feels both familiar and new. We’re excited to continue it together, one page at a time.

Travel Guide

We’re so excited you’re making the journey to celebrate with us!
We hope this guide helps make your journey smooth and comfortable.

Visa Information (International Guests) :

  • Most international will require a Tourist Visa to enter India.
  • E-Visa facilities are available and are valid for 60 days from the date of arrival
  • Please ensure your passport is valid for at least six months from your travel date.
  • Local SIM cards and currency exchange counters are available at Bangalore International Airport.

About Nature Knots :
Nature Knots is a peaceful, nature-inspired resort and event venue, surrounded by greenery, open spaces and calm countryside views. A perfect setting to relax and celebrate together!

Places to visit near Nature Knots :

  • Hesaraghatta Lake – peaceful sunsets and open views
  • Hesaraghatta Grasslands – perfect for nature walks and photography
  • Local countryside – relaxed strolls and quiet moments close to the venue

Places to visit in Bangalore city :

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Cubbon Park
  • Nandi Hills – a lovely day trip for scenic views and fresh air

Weather in December

  • December in Bangalore is pleasant and mild
  • Daytime temperatures range between 20–26°C (68–79°F)
  • Evenings can be cooler, so we recommend carrying a light jacket or shawl
  • Comfortable, breathable clothing is ideal for daytime events

General travel tips :

  • App-based taxis such as Uber and Ola are reliable and widely available.
  • Travel time from Bangalore International Airport to Nature Knots is approximately 1–1.5 hours.
  • Credit and debit cards are widely accepted in the city.
  • UPI and digital payments are very common in India.
  • Carry some cash for small purchases, local shops, or tips.
If you need any assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out, we’re happy to help!
